## system_info
The user's standard directories (Desktop, Documents, Pictures, Videos) are located inside OneDrive (`~/OneDrive/Desktop`, `~/OneDrive/Documents`, etc.). Whenever you need to access or list the Desktop, ALWAYS use `~/OneDrive/Desktop` instead of `~/Desktop`, otherwise you will hit permission and config errors.
